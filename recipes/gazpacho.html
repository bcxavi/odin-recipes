Gazpacho

Gazpacho is a traditional Spanish cold soup made from fresh vegetables like tomatoes, peppers, and cucumbers. It is especially popular during the summer because it is refreshing, light, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Green pepper
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Bread (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the tomatoes, cucumber, and green pepper.
  2. Peel the garlic and remove seeds from the pepper.
  3. Add all the vegetables into a blender.
  4. Add olive oil, vinegar, salt, and bread if using.
  5. Blend everything until smooth.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  7. Chill in the fridge for at least 1-2 hours.
  8. Serve cold.
