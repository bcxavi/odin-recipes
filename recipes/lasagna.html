Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel, and melted cheese. It is one of the most popular comfort foods around the world.


Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dried Oregano
  • Lasagna Noddles
  • Egg
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan with chopped onion and garlic until browned.
  2. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and oregano. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg.
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers.
  6. Finish with mozzarella and parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the oven at 180°C (350°F) for about 30-40 minutes.
  8. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
