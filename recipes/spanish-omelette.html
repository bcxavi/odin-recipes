Spanish Omelette

Spanish omelette, also known as tortilla de patatas, is a traditional Spanish dish made with eggs, potatoes, and onion. It is simple, delicious, and commonly served as a tapa or main dish.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the potatoes and onion.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the potatoes and onion until soft.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and add salt.
  4. Drain the potatoes and onion, then mix them with the eggs.
  5. Pour the mixture into a pan and cook on medium heat.
  6. Flip the omelette carefully and cook the other side.
  7. Cook until fully set but still tender inside.
  8. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
